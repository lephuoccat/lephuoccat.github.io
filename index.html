About Me

Cat P. Le is a first-year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University. He is a research assistant under the supervision of Dr. Vahid Tarokh. He graduated Summa Cum Laude from Rutgers University with a bachelor’s degree, and California Institute of Technology with master’s degree, both in Electrical Engineering. His previous research focused on Signal Processing and Communications. Prior to coming to Duke, Cat worked at Motorola Solutions as a system engineer in an automated license plate reader project. His current research interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Signal Processing, and Applied Statistic
